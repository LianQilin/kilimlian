Vitality is a state of spirited energy and lifestyle for me. I like the dynamic rhythm of a volleyball match, where every spike and teamwork ignites adrenaline. I also enjoy the rhythmic stride of jogging, where each step invigorates the body and clears the mind. While in the freedom of cycling, I could feeling the wind against your face and the rush of adventure in my veins.

I have to say that, vitality is not just about physical activity, it is about embracing life with enthusiasm in the active exploration of our surroundings.

활력은 단순히 신체 활동에 국한되지 않습니다. 이는 주변을 활기차게 탐험하며 삶을 열정적으로 받아들이는 태도입니다.

Although having an Eastern palate, I enjoy incorporating coffee and bread, both western import, into my daily routine.

I've been particularly fond of home-brewed coffee, where grinding, tamping, and extracting create an aromatic atmosphere that fills the room. The slightly bitter and acidic taste of coffee instantly brings a sense of clarity, far beyond the effects of caffeine itself. As for bread, it offers a dual experience of visual delight and taste satisfaction, providing both a fulfilling staple and adding a touch of unexpected joy to my life.
